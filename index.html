About Me

My name is Jessica Lane, and I got my start with coding when I was fifteen. Back then I was creating skins for jcink forums. It didn't take long to realize that coding was something that I loved, and I was good at. I began my schooling with the Coding Bootcamp through the University of Utah, and should be done with the program in Nombember of 2022.

In my free time I love to read, write, and travel. I've always dreamed of making some big move, and getting a chance to see the world. I like a challenge, and I love seeing when some challenging piece of code starts working.
